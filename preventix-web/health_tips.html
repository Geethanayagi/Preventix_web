Health Tips for Diabetes & Hypertension

Early detection and healthy lifestyle choices can prevent severe complications from diabetes and high blood pressure. Learn simple steps to stay healthy and in control.

Eat a Balanced Diet

Focus on fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and low-salt meals. Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.

Stay Physically Active

Engage in at least 30 minutes of moderate activity like walking, cycling, or yoga most days of the week.

Monitor Regularly

Keep track of your blood pressure and glucose levels. Regular monitoring helps catch risks early.

Visit Your Doctor

Schedule regular check-ups. Professional guidance helps manage risks and adjust treatments on time.
