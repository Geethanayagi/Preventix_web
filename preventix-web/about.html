About Our AI Health Model

Empowering people with early prediction of Diabetes, Hypertension & Heart disease

Our Mission

Diabetes and hypertension are two of the most common lifestyle-related conditions worldwide. When left unmanaged, they can lead to serious complications such as heart disease, kidney failure, and stroke.

Our AI-powered model is designed to predict the likelihood of developing these conditions using patient data, risk factors, and evidence-based insights. By supporting early detection, we aim to empower individuals and healthcare professionals to take preventive action before complications arise.

Key Features of Our Model

Risk Assessment

Analyzes patient history and lifestyle factors to estimate risk levels.

Patients: 0%

Hospitals: 0%

Doctor Friendly

Acts as a decision-support tool, helping clinicians in preventive care.

Preventive Insights

Provides actionable tips on lifestyle and diet to reduce risks.

How It Works

Step 1: Patient shares basic health information and lifestyle details.

Step 2: The AI model analyzes the data and identifies risk factors.

Step 3: The system provides a personalized risk score and insights.

Step 4: Doctors and patients use the results to take preventive action.
